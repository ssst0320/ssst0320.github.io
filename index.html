Welcome to Xing Sheng Research Group!

    We aim to exploit fundamental light-material interactions and utilize them to design, fabricate and integrate advanced electronic and photonic materials, structures, devices and systems. Our recent works include the development of high-performance optoelectronic devices (solar cells, photodetectors, LEDs, lasers, etc), targeted at various applications like solar energy harvesting, communication and healthcare. We are currently focused on combining microscale photonic devices with biomedical systems.
    We welcome highly self-motivated students and postdocs with electrical engineering, materials science or physics background to join us!
